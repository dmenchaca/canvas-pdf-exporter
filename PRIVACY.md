# Privacy Policy for Canvas PDF Exporter

**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview

Canvas PDF Exporter is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates entirely on your local device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- ❌ No personal information is collected
- ❌ No browsing history is tracked
- ❌ No user data is stored
- ❌ No analytics or tracking tools are used
- ❌ No cookies are set
- ❌ No data is transmitted to external servers

## How the Extension Works

Canvas PDF Exporter operates entirely locally in your browser:

1. **Screenshot Capture**: The extension captures visible screenshots of Canvas e-learning pages you are viewing
2. **Local Processing**: All image processing and PDF generation happens locally on your device
3. **Local Storage**: The generated PDF is saved directly to your computer's download folder
4. **No External Communication**: No data leaves your browser or device

## Permissions Explanation

The extension requires certain Chrome permissions to function. Here's why each permission is needed:

### activeTab Permission
- **Purpose**: To capture screenshots of the current tab you are viewing
- **Usage**: Only activates when you click the extension icon and press "Download as PDF"
- **Scope**: Limited to the active tab only, no access to other tabs

### scripting Permission
- **Purpose**: To interact with Canvas SCORM content within iframes
- **Usage**: 
  - Navigate between course pages automatically
  - Temporarily hide navigation elements during capture
  - Detect total number of pages
  - Optimize page appearance for PDF export
- **Scope**: Limited to the current Canvas page you are exporting
- **Data Access**: No user data is read, collected, or transmitted

## Third-Party Libraries

The extension uses jsPDF, an open-source JavaScript library for PDF generation:
- **Bundled Locally**: The library is included in the extension package
- **No Remote Code**: No code is loaded from external sources
- **Offline Operation**: Works completely offline

## Data Storage

The only data stored by this extension:
- **User Preferences**: Your quality, resolution, and delay settings are stored locally in Chrome's storage API
- **Location**: Stored only on your device
- **Purpose**: To remember your preferences between uses
- **Access**: Only accessible by the extension, not shared with anyone

## Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted:
- There is no risk of data breaches
- Your course content remains private
- All processing happens in your browser's secure sandbox environment

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
